Splitgate

Displayed below are short videos of my friends and I playing Splitgate. Splitgate is the newest of the games used for Unpopular Clips. Released in 2019, the game took a while to become relatively popular, but since its release it has taken the gaming community by storm. It has the feel of classic Halo and the mechanics of a great puzzle game called Portal. The game itself is still in beta and getting better all the time. The developers making the game have done a great job at elimination bugs and glitches very quickly. Cosmetically, the game could be better but it's getting there. The player base and community around the game is also great. Everyone loves the refreshing feel of this game and how different it is to Call of Duty and other first-person shooters.

Unpopular Clips provides you with a realistic view of Splitgate; played by mediocre, average, and decent everyday gamers. Nothing crazy, but definitely nothing you have seen before!
