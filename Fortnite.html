Fortnite

Below are short videos of my friends and I playing Fortnite the past few years. When I aksed what clips they would like me to include they sent these. Fortnite is on of my favorite games and it continues to impress and entertain every time I pick it up to play it. The reason being is how diverse they have made the game. There are thousands of player-made game modes to play now and more are created everyday. There is something for every gamer. This game was the biggest game in the world for a few months in 2018, since then it has had a slight fall from grace. I always tell people who stoppped playing it to pick it up again because it has changed so much. They have also collaborated with every big franchise you can think of. They have cosmetic items from big movies like Alien or Terminator, to Spider-Man and Iron-Man. They have items from Nike and Adidas, and even real people like Ariana Grande and Bruno Mars. No other game offers this kind of customization and it really adds to the whole experience.

Unpopular Clips provides you with a realistic view of the game today from bad players all the way to half decent players. Guarenteed like nothing you have seen before. Enjoy!
