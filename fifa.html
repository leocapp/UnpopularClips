FIFA: Soccer


Videos from the digital version of the wolrd's greatest sport





















FIFA

FIFA is more than just a video game. It is the digital version of the World's biggest sport. It is also EA Sports' biggest game. The game itself relies on real world players. Real time updates happen week after week as players do well or do poorly during their season. You can track you favorite players throughout the season and see how they look on the game. Real life events are even portrayed in the game flawlessly. The World Cup has its own mode in FIFA and when the tournament comes around, the game has so much extra content to mess around with. And after every soccer season starts in the Fall, they release the next year's edition of the game, each one slightly better than the last with new features and smarter AI.

These are short videos from FIFA through the years. You will see some crazy goals, some terrible defending and shocking goalkeeping, but you have never seen any of it before.
