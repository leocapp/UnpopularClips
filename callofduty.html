Call of Duty

The Call of Duty series has sold more copies than any other series of games ever. They usually release a new game each year and every year it follows a different theme. Mostly centered around war, their latest release is Call of Duty: Vanguard and before that Call of Duty: Cold War and Modern Warfare. There is so much talent in these games, the player base is so dedicated to their craft. It remains at the top of charts for most played game and most watched game year after year. Of all the first-person shooters, Call of Duty is the whole package.

There is plenty of room for crazy clips in Call of Duty, most of which you can find all over Instagram and YouTube. Unpopular Clips has a bunch of videos you have never seen.
